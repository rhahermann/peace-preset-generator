# Headphone Profile: Sennheiser HD 4.40 BT

## Target Headphone
**Model:** Sennheiser HD 4.40 BT (Bluetooth Wireless)

## Native Sound Signature

### Frequency Response Characteristics
- **Overall**: Consumer-friendly V-shaped signature
- **Bass Response**: Elevated, especially in sub-bass (20-60 Hz) - warm and punchy, can be slightly bloated
- **Midrange**: Recessed around 1-3 kHz - vocals and instruments sit back in the mix
- **Treble**: Moderate boost around 6-10 kHz for clarity, not harsh or sibilant
- **Tuning Philosophy**: Fun, engaging sound for casual listening (not studio/reference)

### Bluetooth Considerations
- **Codecs**: SBC, AAC (standard Bluetooth codecs)
- **Impact**: Slight roll-off in extreme highs, minor dynamic compression vs wired
- **Latency**: Present but acceptable for music listening

## Compensation Strategy (Based on User Sample)

The user's proven baseline preset ("Bluetooth Headset 1.peace") demonstrates:

1. **Sub-bass boost (+8 → +5.5 dB)**: Adds punch without excessive bloat
2. **Controlled bass taper (+3 → +0.5 dB)**: Prevents muddiness in upper bass
3. **Heavy mid scoop (-1 → -4 dB)**: Embraces the headphone's natural recession for spacious, atmospheric sound
4. **Rising highs (-1.5 → +5 dB)**: Compensates for Bluetooth roll-off and adds air/sparkle

## User Listening Preferences
- **Bass**: Wants to "feel the kick" - strong sub-bass emphasis
- **Mids**: Prefers atmospheric, ethereal sound over vocal presence - embraces the scoop
- **Highs**: Appreciates sparkle and air without harshness
- **Overall**: Spacious, immersive, not analytical or vocal-forward

### Genre Adaptations
- **Electronic/Trance**: Keep or enhance sub-bass, maintain atmospheric scoop, boost highs for sparkle
- **Metal**: Strong sub-bass for kick drums, aggressive mid-scoop for clarity, sharp highs for cymbals
- **Ambient/Chill**: Smoother curve, gentle bass, deep atmospheric scoop, soft highs

## Technical Specifications
- **Frequency Range**: 18 Hz - 20,000 Hz
- **Impedance**: 32 Ω (easy to drive)
- **Driver**: Closed-back dynamic
- **Battery Life**: ~25 hours
- **Weight**: ~225g
